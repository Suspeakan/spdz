СЫПЕҖ was created by Suspeakan, the writer of this page. I made the language in early summer 2023 with the idea of making a conlang that, in retrospect, haphazardly combined aspects of multiple real world languages into one, but the largest part would come from Russian and very early on the language was switched to Cyrillic (КУРИЛИКУСАМ).

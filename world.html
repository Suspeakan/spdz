ЛАС РИЯР СЫПЕҖ ТИСМИ Conlang Fandom
One quick thing to note is that, Sépedz was made first; the world its in is not held together and is meant purposefully to be vague. There is no worldbuilding that effectively hasn't been built on the spot, so adjust your expectations! This conlang was more about 1) the language itself; 2) impacts on language by the construction of the socialist[/communist]* mode of production.

Sépedz, natively СЫПЕҖ, or, поход парха сыпеҗ, is the language of полидёнэца (polidjònàca), meaning "common nation" (geographical), the term used by the сыпеҗ лоз́ецы (Sépedz Lozjecí) (nationality and autonym), the citizenry of the НЭЦЁ ПУПУЛАРАЙК ПОЛАШЁ ШУНОДЁ (Nàcjò Pópularagk Polashjò Shónodjò) (nation), shortened to НЭЦЁ ППШ (NPPS), or нэцё пепеше informally, a Socialist nation born out of the нэц доликомкамотҳой-ко малсё пулашё (nàc dolikomkamotháj-ko malsjò pólashjò) or the долкамтмалпа (dolkamtmalpa), which may be translated as "(the) Great Proletarian Revolution" ("revolution" more accurately being translated as "advancement", otherwise and for other revolution[s], the loanword редолуция is used)—which was a, as in the name, proletarian (that being, workers') revolution to topple the хойз́ара (Xájzjara), a constitutional monarchical government; and was replaced by the Revolutionary Socialist government, which were lead by the САПЕР ХОСРОСЫПЁ—ПОЛПАР (SAPER XOSRÓSÍPJÒ-POLPAR, "Red Party—POLPAR").
